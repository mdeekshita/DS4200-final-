This is a graph that shows the top 10 complaints over the years and how they increased or decreased. You have the option to choose between what complaint you want to see in detail or you can choose to see all of the complaints over time. Some of the complaints, such as crowding, are broken up into segments. This suggests that crowding was a major problem for a couple of years, and then it was not, but then it got back to being a huge problem.
